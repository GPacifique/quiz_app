Maths Syllabus

Core Topics

  • Number sense and operations (contextualized with African trade and markets)
  • Algebra (patterns in African art and music)
  • Geometry (African architecture and design)
  • Measurement (local units, land, and resources)
  • Data handling (statistics with local data)
  • Problem-solving (real-life African scenarios)

Special Focus

  • Financial literacy: Saving, budgeting, and entrepreneurship in African communities
  • Math in African games and sports (e.g., Mancala, football statistics)
  • Math and technology: Mobile money, digital payments
